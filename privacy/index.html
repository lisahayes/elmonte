Privacy Policy · Effective July 2026

Privacy Policy

This site is intentionally simple: it has no contact forms, no user accounts, and no checkout. The little data involved in using it is described below in plain language. Questions go to (626) 898-6169.

What This Site Collects

elmonteleakrepairpros.com is a static informational website. It does not offer forms, logins, comments, or payment processing, so it never asks you to type personal information into a page. Browsing the site works without providing any identity at all.

Like nearly every website, our hosting infrastructure may record standard server logs: the IP address of a request, the page requested, the time, and the browser type. These logs exist for security and performance and are not used to build profiles of visitors.

Phone Calls

The way to contact us is by phone at (626) 898-6169. When you call, we receive the caller ID information your carrier transmits, plus whatever details you choose to share with the dispatcher, such as your address and the plumbing symptom. That information is used to schedule and perform the service you requested, and for related records like invoices and warranty documentation.

Calls to our number may be routed through a call-management platform and may be recorded or monitored for quality, dispatch accuracy, and training. If a recording notice plays at the start of a call, continuing the call constitutes consent under California law. If you prefer not to be recorded, say so at the start and the dispatcher will accommodate where possible.

Cookies and Analytics

This site may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to count page views and understand which pages help visitors, and standard measurement may note that a visit led to a phone call. We do not run advertising trackers, sell visitor data, or share browsing information with data brokers. You can block cookies in your browser without losing any function of this site.

How Information Is Shared

Details you provide by phone are shared only as needed to complete your service: with the licensed technicians performing the work, with the call-routing platform that connects your call, and where the law requires it. We do not sell or rent customer information to anyone. Ever.

Your Choices

California residents can ask what information we hold about them, request correction, or request deletion, subject to records we must keep for legal, warranty, or tax reasons. Make any of these requests by calling (626) 898-6169 and asking for a privacy request, or by mailing El Monte Leak Repair Experts, 5680 Main St, El Monte, CA 91731.

Children

Our services and this website are directed at property owners and managers, not children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices change, this page changes with them, along with the effective date above. Significant changes will be visible here rather than buried elsewhere.
