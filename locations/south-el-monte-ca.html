The Residential Meter Check

The meter test works the same regardless of city limits. Note the meter reading, then avoid all water use for half an hour and check again. Movement means a pressurized supply leak somewhere in the system. Bring that reading to (626) 898-6169 and the diagnostic visit starts already focused on supply rather than drain or yard.

South El Monte Questions

Do you pull permits in South El Monte or only El Monte?

Both. We pull permits from whichever jurisdiction the job requires, and South El Monte has its own building and public works departments that issue them. My South El Monte warehouse has recurring drain clogs. Is that a plumbing problem?

Likely both a maintenance and a condition problem. Grease-heavy commercial drains clog on schedule when they are not jetted regularly, but recurring clogs at the same location also signal a structural issue, a belly, a root entry, a corroded section. A camera inspection pairs the clog solution with the condition read, and we schedule it around your facility's hours.
