The Fitting Patrol

It is an hour of prevention tuned exactly to what actually fails here, and it bundles with any other visit for next to nothing.

Aztec Hollow Questions, Answered

Is a newer El Monte home really at risk of leaks?

At different risks, on a different clock. The runs themselves are usually mid-life and sound; the fittings, valves, hoses, and connections around them age faster and fail first. A newer home's leak is typically small, accessible, and cheap when caught early, which is exactly why the periodic fittings check matches this housing better than worry does.

My angle stop will not turn. Should I force it?

No. A seized multi-turn stop that gets muscled often snaps its stem or starts weeping at the packing, converting a maintenance note into an active leak. Leave it, plan to use the house main if that fixture ever needs isolating, and have the stop swapped for a quarter-turn during the next visit. It is a minutes-long job done properly.
