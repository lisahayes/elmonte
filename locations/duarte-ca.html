Duarte Plumbing Questions

My Duarte foothill home has moisture at a stem wall. Leak or hillside drainage?

On a foothill property, both are real possibilities, and the answer decides the fix entirely. Supply and drain tests clear or convict the plumbing in one visit. If plumbing tests clean, the moisture source is drainage, and the correct remedy is grading or drainage work rather than pipe repair.
