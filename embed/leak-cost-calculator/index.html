Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a dripping faucet cost per year in El Monte?

A slow drip of about 10 drips per minute loses roughly 50 gallons per month. At San Gabriel Valley Water Company rates (approximately $7.47 per CCF), that is around $0.50 per month or about $6 per year. A fast drip at 120 drips per minute costs about $7 per month, or nearly $90 per year. Use the calculator above with your own provider selected for an accurate figure. Our faucet leak repair page covers the repair options. Call (626) 898-6169 to schedule same-day repair.

Which water company serves my El Monte address?

El Monte has up to nine water providers depending on exact address. San Gabriel Valley Water Company serves the majority of El Monte. The City of El Monte Water Department serves about 17 percent of residents. Golden State Water Company, La Puente Valley County Water District, and others cover the remainder. The provider name appears on your monthly or bimonthly water bill. The City of El Monte Water Department bills bimonthly (every two months) rather than monthly, which changes the math for cost comparisons.

Does a running toilet raise the water bill?

Yes, significantly. A running toilet typically passes 200 to 700 gallons per day depending on the severity of the flapper failure. At local rates, that can add $15 to $50 per month or more. It shows up entirely on the water bill because toilet waste goes to the sewer, but El Monte sewer is a flat annual charge so it does not compound. A silent toilet is the single most common cause of unexplained water bill spikes in El Monte homes. Visit our toilet leak page for repair options, or call (626) 898-6169 for same-day service.

Does El Monte have tiered water pricing?

Yes, for most providers. San Gabriel Valley Water Company and the City of El Monte Water Department both use tiered structures where higher usage triggers a higher per-unit rate. This calculator uses Tier 1 base rates, which are accurate for normal residential usage. If your leak pushes you into Tier 2, your actual cost will be higher than the estimate shown. Call (626) 898-6169 to confirm the rate structure for your address.
